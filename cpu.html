CPU- Central Processing Unit

How does a CPU works?

A CPU, or Cemtral Processing Unit, executes the results abck in memory. This process involves various components like Arithematic Logic Unit (ALU) for mathematical and logical operations, Control Unit for instruction interpretation and sequencing, Register for temporary storage, and buses for data transfer.

Different types of CPU.

There are several types of CPUs including:
1.General Purpose CPUs: These are versatile processors found in most computers, capable of handling a wide range of tasks.
2.Mobile CPUs: Designed for smartphones, tablets and other portable devices, these CPUs priotirize power effeciency and compact size.
3.Server CPUs: Optimized for handling heavy workloads in data centers and servers environments, emphasizing multi-core configurations and high throughout.

What type of CPU needs for gaming?

For gaming, you generally want a CPU that offers strong single-core performance since many games rely heavily on this aspect of CPU performance. A multicore CPU with high clock speeds is also advantageous for handling various tasks simultaneously while gaming, such as background processes and system overhead.
